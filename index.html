How to order products on your website?

To order products on our website, simply select the item of interest from the catalog, add it to your cart, and proceed to checkout, providing the necessary delivery and payment details. Upon completion of this process, you will receive a confirmation of your order to the email address you provided.

How to find out about the latest promotions and discounts?
  1. Subscribe to our newsletter to receive notifications of all current offers to your email.
  2. Regularly visit our website and pay attention to banners or special sections where we announce our promotions and discounts.
  3. Follow our official pages on social networks, where we also announce our current promotions and special offers.
What are the delivery times?

Delivery times depend on your location and the chosen method of delivery. Typically, delivery takes between 3 to 7 business days for domestic orders and 7 to 14 business days for international orders. Please note that these times may vary depending on delivery conditions and other factors.

How to track my order?

To track your order, follow these steps:

  1. Login to your account on our website using your credentials.
  2. Go to the "My Orders" or "Order History" section.
  3. Find the desired order in the list and click on it to view detailed information about the order.
  4. If available, find the tracking number of your order and use it to track the delivery status on the courier's website.
  5. If you have any additional questions or issues with tracking, feel free to contact our customer support service.
How to return or exchange items?

Provide proof of purchase at the specific store. The best way is with a receipt. Write a statement for the exchange or return of the item. If the seller agrees to refund, demand an act or invoice for the return.
